Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Meta Agency Ad Account? +

A Meta Agency Ad Account is a specialized advertising account provided through Meta's Business Manager for agencies and businesses. It offers enhanced features, higher spending limits, and better support compared to standard ad accounts. Watch the video for a complete explanation.

Who can use it? +

Meta Agency Ad Accounts are designed for businesses, agencies, and professionals who need advanced advertising capabilities. This includes digital marketing agencies, e-commerce businesses, service providers, and companies running large-scale advertising campaigns. The video explains eligibility in detail.

How does the setup process work? +

The setup process involves several steps including business verification, account configuration, and integration with your existing advertising infrastructure. We guide you through each step. Please watch the complete video first to understand the full process before contacting us.

How do I contact support? +

After watching the video and understanding the requirements, you can contact us via WhatsApp using the button on this page. Please ensure you've watched the complete video first, as it answers most common questions and prepares you for the conversation.
